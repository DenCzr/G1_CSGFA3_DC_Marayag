The project is about helping people who are starting their business in different parts of the world. These are the researches that I put together in order to make the statistic. Basically the Trade balance tells us the difference in value over a period of time between a country's imports and exports of goods and services, and the trade growth rate tells increase or decrease in the total value of goods and services exchanged between countries over a specific period. For the Qualitative data, the import tariffs tells us that most of the country's import Tariffs are at moderate, and the pie chart tells us that most of our data comes from the continent "Asia".
